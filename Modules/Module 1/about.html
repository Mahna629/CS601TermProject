Where I am from

Image from: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fairport_Lift_Bridge

I have lived my whole life, aside from the 4 years I spent doing my undergradute studies in Ithaca, NY at Ithaca College, in Fairport, NY, a suburb about 15 minutes outside of Rochester.

Perhaps the most notable thing about Fairport is the Lift Bridge crossing the Erie Canal (pictured above), which is unique across the world in its angeled design. This fact is a point of pride for many people in Fairport. Unfortunatly, the bridge is currently out of commmission for refurbishment, but luckily I haven't had to go much of anywhere recently.

Like I touched on above, I went to Ithaca College for my undergradute studies, and studied Screenwriting, with minors in Video Game Development and Medieval and Renaissance History.

Below is an image of part of the Ithaca College campus, with the city of Ithaca and Cayuga lake in the distance. Not a bad sight to see everyday.

What I do and what I would like to do

I work at Best Buy, specifically in the Home Theater department, selling TVs and audio solutions. However, I am currently furloughed due to COVID-19.

As a result, recently I have been spending a lot of time with my studies, and in my free time I have been working through the mountain range of unread books, unplayed games, and unwatched shows and movies I've accumulated over the years. You can find a table of works I have been enjoying recently on the extras page.

I have also been trying to get back on track with some creative projects, since my dream ever since I discovered The Lord of the Rings in second grade has been to write a fantasy novel.

Something else I enjoy, and would like to do more of someday, is travel. I have been to 37 US states, including Alaska, as well as Italy and the British Isles. My favorite place I have visited would probably by Florence, Italy, but that might only be because of the pear ravioli I ate there, which is without a doubt the most delicious thing I have ever eaten.

For the future, Japan, Hong Kong, and Dubai are probably the three places I want to visit most.
